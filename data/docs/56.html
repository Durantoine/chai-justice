Report: Addressing Ethical Dilemmas in AI: Listening to Engineers Report

  1. Home
  2. Focus Areas & Initiatives
  3. Autonomous and Intelligent Systems (AIS)
  4. Report: Addressing Ethical Dilemmas in AI: Listening to Engineers Report

This report is based on the proceedings of the online hackathon “Ethical dilemmas in AI – engineering the way out”, conducted in September 2020 by the Association of Nordic Engineers (ANE), the Data Ethics ThinkDoTank (DataEthics.eu), IEEE and the researchers from the Department of Computer Science at the University of Copenhagen. The goal of the hackathon was to identify the main challenges for integrating existing ethical principles and guidelines into the engineering processes that power AI development.
